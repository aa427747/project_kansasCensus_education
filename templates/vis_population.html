Kansas County Populations

Correlations Discovered Between Population and Income

There are 2 large counties in Kansas that have created outliers that distorts the graph. Many of the counties in Kansas relatively have the same population and the median income appears to be relatively the same. Most of the counties have a population under 10,000 and median income between $25,000 - $40,000 in 2017. The issue we did see is that the metro counties, do have a higher poverty rate relative to the other counties.
